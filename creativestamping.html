Stamp Options  - Creative Stamping

These are some examples of what's called creative stamping.  This is where we take our stamps and make unique patterns with them.  We will be adding to this page as we do more along these lines.  We'd like to thank Paul and Karen Burnett of Painting Cow Studios for taking the time to write some books and tuturials on this method of leather decoration.

When we do a particular design for a customer we try our best to preserve that design for that customer unless it's a design we have done for a series.  So please keep in mind that the examples below are just to show you a small sample of what's possible. For your case we will create a unique pattern and not copy what we have done previously.
