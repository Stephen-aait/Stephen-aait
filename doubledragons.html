A Different Sort of Case
For this case we returned to a popular style, the butterfly case.  This case is called the butterfly because it opens lengthwise all the way to lay out flat like a butterfly. Easy.

This style has the advantage of being lightweight and can hold more than twice the amount of cues as a heavier tube case of the same size.

This type of case used to be the case of choice for many dealers as they could carry up to 6-8 cues securely attached to the display boards and up to 15 cues in individual sleeves.
 
This particular case is special as well.  We tooled it with a Chinese dragon theme.  When we were done tooling it though we like the tooling so much that we did not want to cover up the tooling with pockets.  So we borrowed an idea from one of our customers, Jim Sward, and put the pockets inside the case.

This is the top side.  It is tooled with one red and one green dragon in the traditional Chinese pose for dragons.
 

 
Which meant that we needed to be creative in design so we made the pockets detachable so that you can put them whichever way works best for you.  And we designed the boards to protect the cues from the pockets and to protect the tooling on the pockets as well.  And the display boards are also detachable so that you can take them out as needed or switch them around as you like.
 

The case is a 4x6 with one board and 8x12 with two boards.  It is a fully lined with all the protective features that come with all the cases we build.  Moisture resistant super soft liner that won't wear out, double thick foam rubber to protect against impact, industrial strength rivets at the stress points, a heavy duty luggage-grade, self healing zippers, and of course an elastic loop system that cradles your cues gently but holds them securely so they cannot fall out of the case.  The leather is treated to resist soiling and liquids. 

This is a view of the handle side and the bottom.  The handles are reinforced with metal inside and attached with luggage rivets.  The bottom of the case was tooled black on black to resist stains.
