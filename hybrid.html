The Rolls Royce

Hand Made Excellence.

This case is one of the favorites I have made.  It is a hybrid of some of features from the best casemakers around.   And to top it off the case is hand stitched to finish it which means that it could be made without a line of rivets to close the loop on the exterior.  For this case I chose to combine the scallop design from Instroke, use the Jay Flowers/Justis style of top and strap, add some old world luggage handles (like Murnak uses), and borrowed the pocket style of Dennis Swift on the upper pocket.  For an added twist I made a case length inlay of an exceptional Sheridan Carving.  The leather for the main body is super high grade oil leather that has a waxy, oily feel to it.  Very rich and luxurious.  The inlay is vegetable tanned cowhide.  I am very proud of this case as it represents our artistry, craftsmanship, flexibility and adaptability.  




The case is a 2x4 for 2 butts and 4 shafts.  This case features the new JB light weight interior.  The leather covers a hard shell that is fully lined and specially built to work just like an Instroke tube case.  But it's 2lbs lighter.  It is a fully lined tube case with all the protective features that come with the tube cases I build.  Moisture resistant super soft liner that won't wear out, foam rubber to protect against impact, industrial strength rivets at the stress points, a heavy duty closure, and spring loaded cavities.  The leather is treated to resist soiling and liquids. It is signed "John Barton  09-2007 1 of 1"
