Basic and Ultimate Rugged Nylon Cases

This line of cases started out as a desire to build a simple case out of durable nylon that would be inexpensive yet very strong. 

TESTIMONIALS FROM HAPPY CUSTOMERS

We have been testing them out for about a year making and selling them infrequently through our website. Our dealers in China have had great success with them and we have currently made a couple hundred of them.  Soon they will be available in the USA through a few dealers and always available through us directly.  We offer the basic models shown here as is or the cases can be customized according to the price list below.  And as with all of our cases we also offer full custom on request.  Simply tell us what you want and if it's possible we will tell you how much and build it.

We started with basic black and have expanded to a total of 15 colors.  This nylon is 1250D with a waterproof backing.  It's elegant, scratch resistant and very tough.  All of the stress points are reinforced with genuine leather which adds to the beauty but more importantly makes the case very strong where it's important.  The interior is our UltraPad  model which holds the cues snugly and securely.  The bottom has our strong rubber pad imprinted with our logo.  The shoulder strap has a non-slip rubber grip.  The lower pocket has an extra space for up to a 14" jump cue handle.  These cases are also very light due to the nylon being light but tough. 

Overall the cases have been well received by our customers.  See the testimonials at the end of the page.  Thank you very much for considering our cases.
