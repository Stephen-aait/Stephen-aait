Artists

These are artists that we currenty work with and also some that we have worked with.  We specialize in building highly protective and well constructed cases.  We are not artists however and so we are fortunate that there are highly skilled folks who like to work with us to create the case of your dreams.



It is not always an easy process to go from your mind to a finished case when there is a lot of interpretation and a few folks in between you and the case.  But we are committed to bringing you together with artists who understand our medium.  If you are willing to go on a journey then we are willing to go with you to bring your ideas to life.
TESTIMONIALS FROM HAPPY CUSTOMERS

Current partners:
DINO
Dino Copreros - Dino is an accomplished illustrator from the Philippines.  With many orginal comic books and his own style of modern celtic knots Dino is a master of many styles.
DJAY
Djay Latuputty - Djay is a graphic artist and tattoo flash creator from the Netherlands.  He excels at taking our existing cases and mashing them up to create new designs.


STEVE
Steve DeJong - Steve is an amazing artist who is a master of urban art graffiti style, tatoo flash, and has an very original eye for tribal designs.  We are pleased to offer his work as decoration on our cases.  Please see his page for designs that can be appied to any case immediately.
Dali

Dali is an accomplished leather worker from northern China. When he is not skillfully shaping leather he is raising corn.  With a hand always in nature Dali brings a wonderful flow to his designs.
Past partners:

ZHEN HAI
Lee Zhen Hai - Zhen Hai was our first in-house tooler.  Another tattoo artist, he brought his art skills to leather work and in collabroation we created a lot of great cases.  Now Zhen Hai runs his own studio making wallets, purses, belts and everything but cue cases :-)  We think he prefers to deal with easier items to put his great art on. 


GUO JIN
Guo Jin - Guo Jin came to us fresh out of grahic design school.  With us he learned a lot about designing for leather cases and we owe him a lot for the funky designs on most of our Mason cases.  He left us to get married and probably take a job making ads for condos.....but we hold out hope that we will see him again creating beauty on leather.



OTHER ARTISTS
In addition to the artists we worked with consistently we have also been fortunate to have some great artists do one or a few designs for us.  We always welcome any artist who would like to submit work to us for our consideration.
