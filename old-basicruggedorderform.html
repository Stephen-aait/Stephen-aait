Basic Rugged Case.  Custom Order Form

1.  Fill out the form as best you can. 
2.  We will take your details and give you  a quote.
3.  You confirm the specs or make changes and email us back.
4.  We will repeat until you are satsified.
5.  Pay us and we will give you a start date.

About 3-4 weeks after the start date your case will be done.  We will send you  pictures upon completion for your  inspection to make sure we built what you want.
