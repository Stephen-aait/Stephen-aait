Steve De Jong



STEVE
Steve DeJong - Steve is an amazing artist who is a master of urban art graffiti style, tattoo flash, and has a very original eye for tribal designs.  We are pleased to offer his work as decoration on our cases. 

You can contact Steve directly to arrange for a design session or choose from the designs below.

Steve's website is http://sdjdesigns.webs.com/  or by email sjdj1972@gmail.com

Below are some of Steve's orginal designs that can be applied to cases immediately, either laser engraved or hand-tooled.  Just pick the ones you want and the locations on the case and if they fit those spots then we can do them right away on your case.  We will show you a mock-up of the designs on your case sketch.

NEW - Steve is creating new designs that veer away from the tribal look - please view his site for the latest designs we can put on our cases for you.  http://sdjdesigns.webs.com/




The use of these designs are charged on a per design basis. 
You only pay one license for each design not for how many times it is repeated on the case. 
The license prices are as follows, $25 for one, $45 for two and $60 for three, $20 each after three.
