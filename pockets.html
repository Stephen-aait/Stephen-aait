Pockets.  

Generally the standard lengths for pockets have evolved into 15" for the lower pocket and 7" for the upper pocket.  These are by no means set in stone.  At least not with us.  We do NOT charge to make a pocket longer, higher, shorter, etc.....  We only charge more when we have to do something that is a lot more work and we will let you know what that is.  

The types of pockets that we do most of are Box Pockets with 90 degrees corners, Rounded corner pockets, free-form......
