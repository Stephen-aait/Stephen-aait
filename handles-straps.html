Ergonomics, the study of physical comfort, is very important to us.  All of our handles and straps are made so that they are comfortable and easy to use.  We URGE you to look at this aspect very carefully because if you ever have to carry your case by it's handle or on your shoulder for any length of time you will absolutely hate it if you have a handle that cuts into your hand or a strap that cuts into your shoulder.  You will not be able to carry the case for more than a few yards at a time without having to change positions.  With our cases we strive to make them so comfortable that you forget that they are there.

Handles - We make a variety of handle styles from the common luggage styles to custom creations.  Some are designated with the names of other cue case makers because that is easier for our customers to understand not because we have "copied" these makers.  Other than us no cue case maker to our knowledge has invented any new handle solutions.

You  will want to choose which side of the case to put your handle on.  We view the handle position from the point of view of the person and the case being face to face.

Straps - Our straps are easily adjustable and come with shoulder pads.  We make several styles and any of them are available on any case.  For maximum comfort  I recommend the backpack style with the offset d-rings on the back.  This style is the one which I have found to "ride" best on the shoulder.
