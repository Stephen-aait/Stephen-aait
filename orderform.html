1.  Fill out the form as best you can.  Help is here to the right ----------------------->
2.  We will take your details and give you a quote.
3.  Pay us and your order will be entered.
4. We will make you a sketch to confirm the details.
5.  You confirm the specs or make changes and email us back.
6.  We will repeat until you are satsified.
7. We will put your order into the line and get started about 30-45 days after
the order is paid and entered.

Lead time is currently running about 60 days. 

We will send you progress pictures during the build for your inspection to make sure we are doing what you want.  This saves time and headache for everyone.
