The Mason Style

This case was inspired by a fly fishing tool case done by master leather worker Arne Mason.  
As you can see it has a nice simple elegance to it yet is also very handy and versatile.  This case is currently a semi-hard case but we might do a version with a hard-tube interior soon.  As it is though the case supports the cues very very well due to the amounf of padding we put in it.  Essentially each sleeve for one butt and two shafts is itself a case and then we bundle these to create a very light but strong case.  When the cues are placed in the case it takes on a rigid form that is very secure.

Case Models:  Full Custom - Buffalo - Butterfly - J.Flowers - Mason -  Rolls Royce - Scallop - Traditional
